Research Compendium 

A research compendium accompanies a scientific publication by providing data, code, and documentation for reproducing a scientific workflow. They can be published on different platforms using the label (or tag, community, ...) research-compendium (GitHub, Zenodo, OSF) or the term "research compendium" in the description (GitLab).
